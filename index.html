About

I'm Abdelrhman Mostafa Hamed — a junior game developer from Cairo, Egypt, currently enrolled in the 9-Month Interactive Media & Game Development Diploma at ITI Smart Village.

My interest is in the low-level side of game development: writing rendering pipelines from scratch, working directly with physics libraries, and understanding exactly what the engine is hiding from you. That curiosity led me to build a full 3D maze from raw OpenGL and a 2D physics prototype on bare Box2D — before touching Unity at all.

Outside of game dev, I've taught programming to kids, shipped educational WebGL games for kindergarteners, and placed in two ITI game jams. I work well under constraints and enjoy the puzzle of making something feel smooth and responsive with minimal resources.

I'm open to junior game developer roles, freelance projects, and collaborations involving real-time graphics or interactive systems.
